NURTURING EARLY LEARNERS


Education is a fundamental right of every child.

At Be Dauntless Foundation, we aim to provide quality cognitive education as well as a healthy and opportunity-filled lifestyle to underprivileged children, equipping them to be a part of cohesive societies with high productivity and economic growth. We take the SDGs as a resource for molding a child into the culture of equality, diversity, and peace-oriented values so that they can be potential change-makers of tomorrow.







LANGUAGE AND LITERACY


Language and Literacy is essential for better understanding and future endeavours.

At Be Dauntless Foundation, we organise collaborative learning and brainstorming sessions among the children to heighten their curiosity levels through which we aim to create divergent thinking, communication skills, and linguistic capabilities. We recognise the linguistic abilities of the children and encourage them to believe in themselves and their cultural moorings.

ENGAGING IN SOCIO-EMOTIONAL DEVELOPMENT


Respect and love are the basis of growth for an individual.

We, at Be Dauntless Foundation, aim to instill social and emotional values in the students that will help in shaping and maintaining a good character and uphold strong moral values. Strengthening these values further guides them in resolving social-emotional issues and encourage these future leaders to adopt a beneficial character.
